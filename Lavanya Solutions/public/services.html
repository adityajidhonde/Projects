Beauty Packages

(A1) High Definition makeup

With the coming of high definition cameras, your cracks and other lines are exposed to the lens more than ever. To make sure that the bride looks flawless in her wedding pictures, we have the HD make up which gives the bride a natural look.

(A2) Matte makeup

The matte makeup makes your skin radiant with a coolness that provides serenity and calmness. The makeup is a popular one as it is suitable for all skin types. It is also less likely to smear upon touch. Hence a great choice for weddings.

(A3) Waterproof Makeup

Waterproof makeup helps the overall look last longer, especially when there is more humidity or sweat due to high temperatures. It can last through outdoor sports or physical activities all year round — and for that, it’s one of MVP in our collection.

(B1) Shimmer makeup

Shimmer makeup is used to highlight and accentuate areas of skin the light hits. It is a versatile makeup that can be used on people of all ages and skin types. With this makeup, you’ll be sure to make an impression with your gorgeous, glowing looks.

(B2) Natural makeup

The main point about natural makeup is that it enhances your features without being loud with the colours you wear. It’s a ‘second skin’ makeup –  makeup that gives the illusion of good skin, whether you have it or not. It uses the effects of light and dark to create a pleasing look.

(B3) Regular Makeup

The regular makeup is the go to option which is light on your budget and uses the basics of beauty techniques. This is especially useful for short duration events as the makeup is not meant to last very long. On the plus side, since it is one of the simplest techniques, there isn’t much scope to go wrong here.
